Customer Guidelines

Your complete guide to shopping on ShopSphere — from browsing to delivery.

How to Shop on ShopSphere

Creating Your Account

Get started in minutes

  • 1 Click Sign Up and fill in your name, email, and a strong password.
  • 2 Verify your email address to fully activate your account.
  • 3 Log in and save your default delivery address in your profile.
  • 4 Use a real email — order confirmations and updates will be sent there.

Browsing Products

Find exactly what you need

  • 1 Visit the Products page to see all available items.
  • 2 Use category filters or the search bar to narrow results.
  • 3 Click a product to see full images, description, price and stock.
  • 4 Check customer reviews before buying to make informed choices.

Placing an Order

Checkout with confidence

  • 1 Add items to your cart and review quantities carefully.
  • 2 Enter your correct shipping address and phone number.
  • 3 Choose payment — Cash on Delivery or eSewa.
  • 4 Confirm and save your Order ID for future reference.

Payments

Safe & easy transactions

  • 1 COD: Pay in cash when your item arrives — no upfront payment needed.
  • 2 eSewa: Pay digitally via eSewa wallet — quick and fully secure.
  • 3 Never share your eSewa PIN or password with anyone including support.
  • 4 Always keep your transaction ID as proof of payment.

Tracking Your Order

Know where your parcel is

  • 1 Go to My Orders to check the real-time status of all orders.
  • 2 Order flow: Pending → Paid → Assigned → Delivered.
  • 3 Click View on any order to see your delivery person's details.
  • 4 Contact support if your order stays in Pending for over 48 hours.

Reviews & Returns

Your feedback matters

  • 1 After delivery, go to the product page and leave an honest review.
  • 2 Rate from 1–5 stars and describe your experience in the comment.
  • 3 If you received a wrong or damaged item, contact support immediately.
  • 4 Return requests must be raised within 3 days of delivery.

Quick Tips for Customers

Keep Account SecureUse a unique strong password. Never share your login details.
Accurate AddressDouble-check your delivery address before confirming an order.
Check Product PhotosView all images carefully — screen colors may differ slightly from real life.

Track Your SpendingUse My Spending to monitor how much you spend per product.
Check Your EmailOrder confirmations and delivery updates are sent to your registered email.
