Supplier Guidelines

Everything you need to manage your products and grow your sales on ShopSphere.

How to Sell on ShopSphere

Setting Up Your Store

Get your supplier account ready

  • 1 Register and request Supplier Permission from the Permissions page.
  • 2 Wait for admin approval — you'll be notified once access is granted.
  • 3 Complete your supplier profile with accurate business information.
  • 4 Access your Supplier Dashboard to begin adding products.

Adding Products

List your items correctly

  • 1 Go to Products and click Add New Product.
  • 2 Write a clear, accurate product name and description.
  • 3 Upload high-quality images — at least one front-facing photo.
  • 4 Set the correct price, category, and initial stock quantity.

Managing Stock

Keep inventory up to date

  • 1 Regularly update stock levels to reflect actual availability.
  • 2 Set a product to inactive if it's out of stock instead of deleting it.
  • 3 Stock is automatically reduced when a customer's order is marked Paid.
  • 4 Review your stock weekly to avoid overselling.

Handling Orders

Stay on top of customer orders

  • 1 Visit the Orders page to see all purchases of your products.
  • 2 Filter orders by product, city, or amount to manage efficiently.
  • 3 Ensure products are packed and ready when an order is marked Assigned.
  • 4 Coordinate with the delivery team for smooth handoffs.

Tracking Earnings

Monitor your revenue

  • 1 Go to Earnings to see total revenue per product.
  • 2 Filter by date range or product to analyse specific periods.
  • 3 Only Paid and Delivered orders count as confirmed earnings.
  • 4 Monitor pending and cancelled amounts to understand lost revenue.

Managing Reviews

Build your reputation

  • 1 Visit the Reviews page to read all customer feedback.
  • 2 Take note of low ratings — they indicate areas to improve.
  • 3 Consistently high-quality products lead to better reviews and more sales.
  • 4 Never attempt to fake or manipulate reviews — this violates our policy.

Supplier Rules

Do's

  • Upload accurate product descriptions and real images.
  • Keep your stock quantity updated at all times.
  • Respond quickly to order changes and delivery coordination.
  • Price your products fairly and competitively.
  • Pack orders securely to prevent damage during delivery.
  • Use the Earnings page to track your income regularly.

Don'ts

  • Do not list products with misleading descriptions or fake images.
  • Do not oversell products you don't have in stock.
  • Do not attempt to manipulate or fake customer reviews.
  • Do not leave inactive products active — mark them correctly.
  • Do not share your supplier credentials with unauthorised users.
  • Do not ignore low ratings — use them to improve your products.

Quick Tips for Suppliers
