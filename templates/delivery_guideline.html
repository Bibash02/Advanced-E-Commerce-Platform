How to Handle Deliveries

Getting Assigned

When an order comes your way

  • 1 An admin will assign orders to you based on your area and availability.
  • 2 Once assigned, the order status changes to Assigned to Delivery.
  • 3 Log into your dashboard and review all assigned order details.
  • 4 Note the customer's name, phone, address, and city carefully.

Picking Up the Order

Collect from the supplier

  • 1 Contact the supplier if needed to confirm the pickup location and time.
  • 2 Verify the package matches the order — check product name and quantity.
  • 3 Ensure the package is sealed and undamaged before accepting it.
  • 4 Never accept damaged or tampered packages — report to admin immediately.

Delivering the Order

Getting it to the customer

  • 1 Contact the customer before arriving — use the phone number on the order.
  • 2 Deliver to the exact address listed on the order — do not substitute.
  • 3 For COD orders, collect the exact cash amount shown on the order.
  • 4 Hand the package only to the customer or an authorised person at the address.

COD Payments

Handling cash on delivery

  • 1 Check the order amount carefully before collecting payment from the customer.
  • 2 Only collect the exact amount stated — do not accept partial payments.
  • 3 Provide a receipt or confirmation to the customer upon collection.
  • 4 Submit collected payments to the admin as instructed — never delay.

Confirming Delivery

Mark orders as delivered

  • 1 After handing over the package, log into your dashboard immediately.
  • 2 Find the order and mark it as Delivered.
  • 3 Do not mark an order as delivered if the customer was not home.
  • 4 If delivery fails, contact admin and report the reason.

Handling Problems

What to do when things go wrong

  • 1 If a customer is unreachable, try at least 3 times before reporting to admin.
  • 2 If a customer refuses delivery, do not leave the package — return it and report.
  • 3 Never open or tamper with any package under any circumstances.
  • 4 Report any accidents, theft, or loss to admin immediately with full details.

Delivery Rules

Do's

  • Always call the customer before arriving at the delivery address.
  • Deliver within the timeframe given by the admin.
  • Handle all packages with care — treat them as your own.
  • Collect exact COD amounts and report all payments promptly.
  • Mark orders as delivered immediately after handoff.
  • Maintain professional and respectful conduct with customers at all times.

Don'ts

  • Do not open, tamper with, or inspect the contents of any package.
  • Do not mark an order as delivered if it was not actually delivered.
  • Do not accept partial payments for COD orders.
  • Do not leave packages unattended at a delivery location.
  • Do not share customer personal information with anyone.
  • Do not delay delivery without informing admin with a valid reason.
