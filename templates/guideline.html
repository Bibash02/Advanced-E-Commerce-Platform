Site Guidelines

Everything you need to know to get the best experience on ShopSphere.

How to Use ShopSphere

Getting Started

Create your account

  • 1 Click Sign Up on the homepage and fill in your name, email, and password.
  • 2 Verify your email address to activate your account.
  • 3 Log in and complete your profile with your delivery address.
  • 4 You're all set — start browsing products!

Browsing & Shopping

Find what you love

  • 1 Use the Products page to browse all available items.
  • 2 Filter by category, price, or search by name to narrow results.
  • 3 Click any product to see full details, images, and description.
  • 4 Add items to your cart and adjust quantities before checkout.

Placing an Order

Checkout smoothly

  • 1 Go to your cart and review all items and quantities.
  • 2 Enter your shipping address and contact details.
  • 3 Choose your payment method — Cash on Delivery or eSewa.
  • 4 Confirm your order — you'll receive a confirmation right away.

Payments

Safe & easy transactions

  • 1 Cash on Delivery (COD): Pay in cash when your order arrives at your door.
  • 2 eSewa: Pay digitally via your eSewa wallet — fast and secure.
  • 3 Never share your payment credentials with anyone.
  • 4 Always save your transaction ID as proof of payment.

Tracking Your Order

Stay updated on delivery

  • 1 Go to My Orders to see the live status of all your orders.
  • 2 Statuses: Pending → Paid → Assigned → Delivered.
  • 3 Click View on any order to see the assigned delivery person.
  • 4 Contact support if your order is stuck in Pending for too long.

Reviews & Returns

Share your experience

  • 1 After delivery, visit the product page to leave an honest review.
  • 2 Rate from 1–5 stars and add a comment to help other shoppers.
  • 3 If you received a wrong or damaged item, contact support immediately.
  • 4 Return requests must be made within 3 days of delivery.

Quick Tips

Keep Your Account Safe Use a strong password and never share your login details with anyone.
Accurate Address Always double-check your delivery address to avoid delays or failed deliveries.
Check Product Images View all product photos carefully before purchasing — colors may vary slightly on screen.
Stay Updated Check your email for order confirmations and delivery notifications.
Cancel Before Dispatch You can cancel a Pending order before it gets assigned to a delivery person.
Track Your Spending Use the My Spending page to keep an eye on your total purchases.
